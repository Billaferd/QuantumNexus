## Narrative Deck Architecture & Rules Integration

The tabulated data represents four strictly regulated, perfectly balanced 200 RP configurations. The inclusion of Terrain explicitly alters the spatial reality of the board, forcing players to calculate movement and deployment through a highly constrained, hostile grid.

The Technology Fortress enforces absolute territorial stagnation. Governed by the Singularity Engine (4 DP) and Blessed Logistics (2 DP), it leverages the rigid geometry of the battlefield to construct overlapping proactive shields. The integration of Jamming Field and Razor-Wire Emplacement Terrain physically denies the Vanguard and Contested Zones to the opponent. Enemy units attempting to breach the 3-Weight capacity limit of a Razor-Wire quadrant are subjected to immediate Impact damage, supplementing the inexorable decay caused by the Vanguard Phalanx. The splash of Divinity provides the Zealous Chaplain, ensuring that the frontline can survive lethal volleys through the Zeal mechanic, which triggers exclusively after the Phalanx defense threshold has been fully exhausted. Reactive Armor upgrades punish aggressive engagements, while Precision Bombardment ensures that escalated go-tall threats are surgically removed by completely bypassing printed Base Defense math.

The Magic Storm relies on localized reality distortion, spatial manipulation, and explosive temporal chaining. Dictated by the Temporal Fracture (4 DP) and Psionic Echo (2 DP) missions, the deck legally splashes low-tier Psionics cards strictly under the 2 DP limit to generate fragile Echo tokens. These tokens abuse the updated Illusion keyword, absorbing single-target removal by forcing attack redirection rather than completely locking the opponent out of the Activation Sequence. The core of the deck weaponizes the physical board through Terrain. By stacking Null-Magic Zones and Aetheric Storms, the player fundamentally alters the rules of engagement in specific quadrants, shielding their fragile Rift Mages while systematically degrading armored enemies. When the spatial grid becomes too congested, Reality Unravels provides the ultimate asymmetrical board wipe. The strict CP costs of these spells demand that the player flawlessly execute 5 CP Reserve banking to afford back-to-back Event sequences retrieved via the Chronomancer’s Arcane Recall.

The Psionics Web operates strictly on systemic degenesis, hand disruption, and tactical paranoia. Bound by Psionic Supremacy (4 DP) and Automated Defenses (2 DP), it bypasses its own physical frailty by locking the opponent in a Mental Labyrinth, a Terrain that strictly prevents any unit within its bounds from executing Move or Tactical Advance actions. Once the enemy is physically pinned, the Neural Architect applies Synaptic Marks to set up devastating executions. Panopticon Relay and Neural Depository Assets secure absolute control over the economic resource trade, punishing the opponent for drawing or discarding cards. The inclusion of Mass Hysteria perfectly executes the strategic re-routing of the Threat-Answer matrix, forcing a mathematically superior enemy formation trapped in a specific quadrant to completely annihilate itself, while the Matriarch Liana proactively strips key answers from the opponent’s hidden hand.

The Divinity Crusade operates as an inevitable, self-fueling engine of attrition. Governed by Absolute Crusade (4 DP) and Standard Spellcraft (2 DP), it completely ignores the concept of tactical retreat. The deck floods the deployment zone with Fanatical Swarms, adhering strictly to the action parity rule that secondary Swarm units must enter the battlefield exhausted. As these units push forward, Consecrated Ground Terrain converts standard quadrants into bastions of holy resilience, artificially granting the Zeal keyword to any unit holding the space. Pillars of Judgment strictly punish the opponent for attempting to contest these sectors. When the frontline inevitably falls, the Cathedral of Martyrs instantly replaces the destroyed bodies with evasive Spirit tokens, and Resurrection Rites cycle the Sanctified Battle-Priests back from the discard pile. Splashed Magic cards like Aetheric Shift provide the rigid formation with crucial Skirmisher mobility, allowing the Crusader Vanguard to hit and reposition defensively while Saint-Queen Elara anchors the final push into the Contested Zone.
